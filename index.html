    About

    The pace of progress in the field of Science & Technology in this millennium has overshadowed the speed of Technological changes in the past decades. In this millennium, this pace would intensify and may even reach a state beyond our imagination. Technical Symposiums like Acumen are an effort to catch up with the pace of technological innovation. Acumen triggered off in the year 1996. It was the first of its kind in Hyderabad and had an overwhelming response. It paved way to Acumen '99, which was a great success, too. Around 180 papers from various parts of the country were received and students from various universities and institutes like IIT, NIT etc. have participated in this symposium. ACUMEN is a one-day festival. The event seeks to achieve communication of innovative ideas that promote the cause of the technological advantage among the students, and also keep them abreast with the latest advances in their respective fields. It also opens a window to the participants to interact with people from different backgrounds and upgrade their knowledge. The theme "YOUTH-TECHNOLOGY-FUTURE" asserts the fact that the future of technology lies in the hands of the youth. This is the time for the students to explore & exhibit their talents in the areas related to academic routine.

    Acumen CIVIL 2019

    theme:

    Biomimicry: Innovation inspired by nature.

    Humans are clever, but without intending to, we have created massive sustainability problems for future generations. Fortunately, solutions to these global challenges are all around us. Biomimicry is an approach to innovation that seeks sustainable solutions to human challenges by emulating nature’s time-tested patterns and strategies. The goal is to create products, processes, and policies—new ways of living—that are well-adapted to life on earth over the long haul. The core idea is that nature has already solved many of the problems we are grappling with. Animals, plants, and microbes are the consummate engineers. After billions of years of research and development, failures are fossils, and what surrounds us is the secret to survival.

    “I think the biggest innovations of the 21st century will be at the intersection of biology and technology. A new era is beginning.”

    -Steve Jobs

    “When we look at what is truly sustainable, the only real model that has worked over long periods of time is the natural world.”

    -Janine Benyus

    You could look at nature as being like a catalog of products, and all of those have benefited from a 3.8 billion year research and development period. And given that level of investment, it makes sense to use it.

    Events

    Here are the events rules and judgement


    Paper Presentation

    Rules

    • TOPIC- Trending issues,problems and solutions in Civil Engineering.
    • Maximum 2 members per team.
    • Last date to submit Abstract by 8 March 2019.
    • Final date of submission of PPT by 12 March 2019.

    Judgement

    • Based on presentation skills , clarity of concepts.
    • Able to give valid answers for the queries.


    Model Presentation

    Rules

    • Theme of the model should be submitted by 8 March 2019.
    • The model might be a working or static.
    • Maximum of 3 members per team.

    Judgement

    • Clear idea about the THEME.
    • Able to explain precisely.
    • Give valid explanations for questions asked by the judges.


    Quiz up

    Rules

    • Pen and paper type.
    • Individual event.
    • It is two round event.1) General civil questions.2) Technical civil questions.
    • Participants who clear the first round are eligible for the next round.

    Judgement

    • Based on their performance


    Surwarriors

    Rules

    • It is a two round event.1)Questions related to surveying.2)Need to survey the given piece of land using surveying instruments.
    • Participants who clear the first round are eligible for the next round.
    • Maximum of 2 members per team.

    Judgement

    • Based on knowledge on subject and accuracy.


    The mighty cube

    Rules

    • Dimensions are released a week before the ACUMEN (7 March 2019).
    • Participants should show their mix design.
    • Maximum of 3 members per team.

    Judgement

    • Based on maximum compressive strength.


    The mighty cube

    Rules

    • Maximum of 3 members per team.

    Judgement

    biomimic

    Rules

    • Prototype of the structure should be built with ingredients like clay etc.
    • A tender for the materials is conducted.
    • Maximum of 3 members per team.

    Judgement

    • Structure constructed with less worth and more aesthetic beauty will be rewarded.
    • Participants should be able to give valid explanations.


    revit omania

    Rules

    • CAD drawings are recreated to REVIT .
    • Participants have to devolop most appealing 3D view using REVIT tools in a limited time provided.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • Based on the outlook of the model.


    CADovation

    Rules

    • Line diagram is provided.
    • Draw plan, elevation and side view in CAD for the given line diagram in given time.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • The chance of winning is more for the participants who complete the task quicker.


    crosswords

    Rules

    • Pen and paper type.
    • It is a two round event.
    • Basic crossword rules.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • Participant who acquire more marks will win.


    great thinker

    Rules

    • Pen and paper type.
    • Need to write solution for the real world problems.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • Based on writing skills.
    • Effective solutions.


    capture the nature

    Rules

    • Need to click pictures related to nature in the college premises.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • The participant who clicks the most delightful picture will be rewarded.


    caption it

    Rules

    • Participants should give a caption for the projected images.
    • It is an individual event.

    Judgement

    • Participants who uses catchy lines or sentences will be rewarded.


    OLYMPIONIXE

    Rules

    • A detailed description along with visual features of an Olympic stadium will be provided. The participant must use his idea and design the given site as asked in the description.
    • A group event of 2 members.
    • A3 or A4 size sheets will be provided at the venue along with appropriate stationary.
    • Time limit of 45mins per session.

    Judgement

    • Concept.
    • Utilizing the space .
    • Explaination.
    • Use of colors.


    URBANSCAPER

    Rules

    • This competition revolves around solving the problems involved in future cities. A detailed description about the scenario will be presented and the participant is required to solve the issue through a sketch.
    • A group event of 2 members.
    • A3 or A4 size sheets will be provided at the venue along with appropriate stationary.
    • Time limit of 45mins per session.

    Judgement

    • Concept and shape.
    • Utilizing the space .
    • Explaination and justification.
    • Use of colors.
    • Understanding the question and presentation.
